ABOUT

The Department of Research of Underground Groove & Sound is the music project of Argentinean producer Cisco Parisi.
Based in Berlin since 2014, Cisco Parisi has been a steady fixture in the underground rock and pop scene in Berlin, from leading the synthpop outfit Minimal Schlager to booking and DJing at 8MM Bar and Loophole, and producing emerging acts such as Fritz Ali Hansen, Hot Couture and Dagur Kaveh.

STUDIO

Located in the heart of Kreuzberg in Berlin, the DRUGS studio is a boutique recording and mixing hub that draws inspiration from the analog techniques of the 70s and the 80s -integrating vintage guitars and synthesizers, world class preamps and rare outboard gear with the latest DAWs and virtual instruments.

The studio is available for production, mixing and mastering.
